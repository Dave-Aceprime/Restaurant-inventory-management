‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add new ingredients to my inventory?

Navigate to the "Add Item" page from the main menu. Fill out the form with ingredient details including name, quantity, category, and storage requirements. Click "Add Ingredient" to save.

Can I track expiration dates for perishable items?

Yes! When adding items, you can mark them as "Highly Perishable" and add expiration notes in the additional notes section. The system will help you prioritize items that need to be used soon.

How do I set up low stock alerts?

When adding or editing an ingredient, set the "Minimum Stock Level" field. When your inventory drops below this amount, the system will automatically flag it as "Low Stock" on your dashboard.

Can I export inventory reports?

Currently, this is a demo version for academic purposes. In the full production version, you'll be able to export reports in PDF, Excel, and CSV formats.

How often should I update my inventory?

For best results, update your inventory daily at the end of service. This ensures accurate stock levels and helps prevent running out of critical ingredients.

Is the system mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! RestaurantPro is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Access your inventory anywhere, anytime.
